How we chose these entries

This collection focuses on science, health, and social studies topics that were still printed in mainstream U.S. middle-school or high-school materials after 1980. Each card lists when the outdated idea was commonly taught, what current evidence says instead, and direct links to the research or institutional announcements that overturned the earlier classroom explanation.

Built by synthesizing peer-reviewed research, museum archives, and government guidance. Want to contribute another correction? Open a pull request!
